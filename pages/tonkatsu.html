Tonkatsu

Description

Tonkatsu is a popular traditional Japanese dish. It is made with pork and panko and is eaten with a special sauce. It is often served with thinly cut cabbage.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Panko
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Remove excess fat from the pork.
  2. Pound the meat until it's tender and reshape towards original shape.
  3. Create a mixture with the eggs and flour.
