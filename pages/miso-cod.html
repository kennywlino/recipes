Miso Cod

Description

Recipe taken from: Just One Cookbook

You might know Miso Cod as Nobu's signature dish. It is a simple dish made by allowing the cod to soak in a mixture of white miso and sake for a few days, and baking or broiling it in the oven. Now you can easily enjoy this dish from the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients

  • Cod fish
  • Salt
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • White miso

Steps

  1. Sprinkle salt over the fish and allow it to sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix white miso, mirin, and sake in a bowl.
  3. Pour some sake over the fish to remove the salt.
  4. Pour the miso mixture into a container.
  5. Place the fish in the mixture and make sure the fish is completely coated.
  6. Allow the fish to marinate for 3 days or so.
  7. When ready to cook, preheat the oven to 400Â°F.
  8. Remove the excess marinate from the fish and place the fish on a lined tray with the skin side up.
  9. Bake for about 20 minutes, checking for browning while baking.
